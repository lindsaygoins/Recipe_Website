MOM'S SPAGHETTI SAUCE

Spaghetti Sauce Ingredients

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-1 1/2 lbs ground beef (adjust amount based on meat preferences)
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 3 (14 oz) cans diced or crushed tomatoes (diced for a chunkier sauce or crushed for a smoother sauce)
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add olive oil. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Add onion and garlic. Cook until onion begins to soften.
  3. Add the tomato paste and stir. Let cook for a few minutes. Do not let the tomato burn.
  4. Stir in the diced or crushed tomatoes, brown sugar, oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, bay leaf, and water.
  5. Bring the sauce to a low boil. Then reduce the heat to medium-low to allow for the sauce to simmer uncovered for approximately 2-3 hours.
  6. Add the black pepper and serve over spaghetti noodles.
